About Me

Hello, and thank you for exploring my portfolio! I am Sai Srikanta Seemaladari, a passionate software developer and tech enthusiast based in Karimnagar, Telangana.

With a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from KITS(S) (72%), and a strong academic foundation from SR College (Intermediate) and Ratnam Global High School, I have always been committed to academic and technical excellence.

Over the years, I have led and contributed to various impactful projects:

  1. Online Slot Booking System: A robust web application built with Python and Django, streamlining sports slot bookings with automated email confirmations and admin-user interactions.
  2. Smart E-Bus Pass: An Android app for managing bus passes with integrated route management, real-time updates, and secure payment gateways, developed using Java and MySQL.

In addition to technical skills, I am proficient in Power BI, SQL, and programming languages like Java and Python. My ability to lead teams and adapt to new challenges has been proven through achievements like organizing a 24-hour hackathon and training peers in advanced tools.

Beyond coding, I actively engage in learning new technologies and mentoring others. My certifications in Data Science, Web Development, and SQL reflect my commitment to growth and excellence in the tech field.

Through this portfolio, I aim to showcase not just my technical expertise but also my enthusiasm for collaborative projects and continuous learning. Feel free to reach out through my LinkedIn or explore my projects on GitHub.
